## 📖 About SomX

SomX is an AI-powered Campus Super App designed to bring every essential student service into one seamless platform.

Instead of switching between multiple apps for notes, attendance, placements, announcements, events, and communication, students can access everything they need in one place.

Our mission is to make college life simpler, smarter, and more connected.

---

## 🎯 Vision

To become the most trusted digital ecosystem for students by connecting academics, campus life, career opportunities, and student communities into one intelligent platform.
